Automated Scan-and-Go End-to-End Simulation Workflow

Step 1: Build a Cart Open the Shopper App on the left. Click on products (e.g., Maggi, Almonds) to simulate scanning them with a beep sound.
Step 2: Pay & Get Receipt Click "Pay via UPI". The app simulates a secure transaction and generates a high-contrast receipt featuring a secure QR token.
Step 3: Auditor Verification On the Auditor App (right), select the transaction from the dropdown (or paste QR). It shuffles and highlights exactly 3 items.
Step 4: Check & Approve Check the physically verified items, then click "Approve Exit". The Shopper App instantly updates to "Exit Approved - Gate Open".
